## Cascade Industrial Supply — Dashboard Remediation & Extension

**Client:** Regional industrial parts distributor, $22M revenue, Portland OR
**Engagement:** 2025 — 8-week fixed-fee

Cascade's internal dashboard had been built by a contractor who left mid-project. We were brought in to audit, stabilize, and extend it. The situation closely mirrors Meridian's: incomplete filter logic, no automated tests, and an operations team working around system gaps rather than through them.

We delivered: defect remediation across three modules, a purchase order recommendation feature for the buying team, and a Playwright test suite covering 14 critical flows. The IT team — who had blocked all changes for six months pending test coverage — approved the first post-engagement update within a week of test suite delivery.

**Technology:** Vue 3, FastAPI, Playwright. Same stack as Meridian.

---

## Fenwick Logistics — Warehouse Operations Modernization

**Client:** Third-party logistics provider, 5 distribution centers across North America
**Engagement:** 2024 — 12-week T&M

Fenwick needed to extend an existing Vue/Python internal tool to support a new facility (Vancouver) and modernize the UI to meet updated brand standards. The engagement included i18n work for their bilingual (EN/FR) Canadian operations team and a dark mode implementation for warehouse floor stations — both relevant to Meridian's D-tier requirements.

Key learning we bring to Meridian: i18n done as a late add creates rework. We recommended — and Fenwick agreed — to implement the i18n infrastructure first, then add the new locale strings incrementally. We will apply the same approach here.

## Thornwick Manufacturing — Operations Dashboard from Scratch

**Client:** Precision parts manufacturer, $18M revenue, Midwest
**Engagement:** 2023 — 16-week fixed-fee

We built Thornwick's inventory and demand forecasting dashboard from greenfield. Relevant to Meridian because the Restocking feature we built for Thornwick — a budget-constrained purchase order recommender based on stock levels and demand data — is functionally similar to R2 in this RFP. That feature has been in production use for 18 months with no defect reports.
