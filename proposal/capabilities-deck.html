Technical Approach — R2

The Restocking View

A new first-class view in the dashboard. R. Tanaka's team enters a budget ceiling; the system returns a ranked purchase order list based on current stock, demand forecast, and cost.

  • Configurable reorder threshold — stock below N days of demand
  • Ranked by priority: highest stockout risk first
  • Budget ceiling respected — recommendations stop when budget is exhausted
  • Respects warehouse and category filters — each site sees their own view
  • Quantity adjustable before acting on recommendations
Data Sources

Uses What's Already There

Current stock from /api/inventory and demand from /api/demand — no new data sources required.

No Scope Creep

In Scope

On-screen recommendations and export to CSV. Out of scope: integration with ERP, procurement APIs, or persistent purchase orders. Those are a follow-on engagement if needed.
